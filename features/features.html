Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to join SheConnect?

Joining SheConnect is completely free. We believe in building a strong, supportive community without barriers to entry. Whether you are a mentee looking for guidance or a mentor willing to share your knowledge, there are no membership fees, hidden costs, or subscriptions involved. Our goal is to create an accessible platform where women can connect, learn, and grow professionally and personally. We are committed to offering a space where financial limitations never stand in the way of opportunity or mentorship.

Do I need to be experienced to become a mentor?

You do not need decades of experience to become a mentor at SheConnect — what matters most is your willingness to guide, listen, and support others. While having some professional experience in your field is certainly valuable, mentorship at SheConnect is about more than just expertise. It's about sharing real-world insights, lessons you've learned, challenges you've overcome, and encouragement. We welcome mentors at all stages of their careers who are passionate about making a difference. Whether you’re an industry veteran or a few years into your journey, your unique perspective could be exactly what someone else needs to hear.

How much time do I need to commit as a mentor?

We understand that mentors have busy schedules, which is why SheConnect offers flexible commitment options. Typically, we recommend mentors dedicate around 2–4 hours per month, but ultimately, it’s up to you and your mentee to decide what works best for both of you. Some mentorships involve weekly check-ins, while others might be monthly conversations, project feedback, or even occasional advice sessions. We encourage quality over quantity — it’s not about the number of hours you spend, but the impact you make during that time. Your availability will be clearly communicated during the matching process, ensuring expectations are set from the beginning.

Can I change my mentor if we're not a good match?

Absolutely — at SheConnect, we believe that the right match is essential for a meaningful mentorship experience. If for any reason you feel that your current mentor or mentee relationship isn't a good fit — whether it's due to communication style, goals, or scheduling conflicts — you can request a rematch at any time. We want every participant to feel comfortable, respected, and empowered in their mentorship journey. Our team is here to support you through the transition process discreetly and respectfully, ensuring you find a better match who aligns with your needs and aspirations.

Is SheConnect only for women in coding roles?

While SheConnect has a strong foundation in tech and coding-related mentorship, we welcome women from a wide range of professional backgrounds. Our community includes mentors and mentees in technology, design, marketing, entrepreneurship, business leadership, product management, data science, and more. We believe that mentorship should be holistic — covering both technical growth and personal development. Whether you're a software developer, aspiring product manager, startup founder, or someone exploring career transitions, you’ll find valuable guidance and support here. At its heart, SheConnect is about connection, empowerment, and growth, no matter what career path you’re on.
