Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

At SheConnect , we understand how important your time and questions are. That's why we make it a priority to respond as quickly as possible. On average, you can expect to hear back from us within 24 hours during regular business days. However, in many cases, we respond within just a few hours, depending on the complexity of your request and our current support load. For urgent concerns, we recommend adding "Urgent" to your message subject for immediate attention. We believe communication should be smooth, reliable, and reassuring. Every message you send matters to us, and we’re committed to providing thoughtful, helpful responses that truly address your needs — not just automated replies. Thank you for trusting us. We’re here to help you every step of the way!

Can I become a partner or sponsor?

Absolutely! We're always looking for organizations that share our mission of empowering women in tech. Our partnership program offers multiple tiers of engagement, from content collaboration to full sponsorship opportunities.

Partners benefit from increased visibility within our growing community, co-branded events, and direct access to our network of female tech professionals. Whether you're a tech company looking to improve diversity, an educational institution, or a non-profit with aligned goals, we'd love to explore collaboration possibilities.

Please specify your interest in partnerships in your message, including details about your organization and how you envision working together. Our partnership team will review your inquiry and respond with potential next steps.

I'm having technical issues with the platform. Who do I contact?

For technical support, please use the contact form and select "Technical Support" in the subject line. Our dedicated tech team will assist you as soon as possible, typically within one business day.

To help us resolve your issue more efficiently, please include the following details in your message:

  • The specific feature or area where you're experiencing problems
  • The device and browser you're using (e.g., iPhone 13 with Safari, Windows PC with Chrome)
  • Screenshots of any error messages you're seeing (if applicable)
  • Steps you've already taken to try to resolve the issue

For urgent technical matters that prevent you from using essential platform features, you can also reach our emergency technical support at support@sheconnect.com.

How do I find the right mentor for my career goals?

Finding the right mentor is crucial to your professional growth. Our platform offers several ways to connect with mentors who align with your career aspirations:

  1. Smart Matching Algorithm: Complete your profile in detail, including your industry, skills, career goals, and specific areas where you'd like guidance. Our algorithm will suggest potential mentors based on compatibility.
  2. Advanced Search: Use our search filters to narrow down mentors by industry, expertise, years of experience, availability, and even mentoring style.
  3. Exploratory Sessions: Many mentors offer free 15-30 minute exploratory calls to determine if there's a good fit before committing to a mentoring relationship.

We recommend reaching out to 2-3 potential mentors initially. In your introduction message, be specific about why you're interested in their guidance and what you hope to achieve through mentorship. This personalized approach typically yields better responses than generic requests.

What is SheConnect's mission and vision?

SheConnect was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge the gender gap in technology by connecting women with the mentorship, resources, and community they need to thrive in tech careers.

Our Mission: To empower women in technology through meaningful mentorship connections, skill development opportunities, and a supportive community that fosters growth, confidence, and career advancement.

Our Vision: We envision a tech industry where women are equally represented at all levels, from entry positions to leadership roles, and where diverse perspectives drive innovation and create better technology solutions for everyone.

Since our founding, we've facilitated over 10,000 mentoring relationships and have seen our members achieve significant career milestones, including promotions, successful career transitions, and launching their own tech ventures. We're committed to continuous growth and improvement as we work toward making our vision a reality.
